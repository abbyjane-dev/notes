On Identity¶

Being a woman means having the capacity to endure asymmetry without complaint.

Before we wrap up, I want to share one personal but relevant detail. I go by Abby Jane. I am transgender, and while some of my materials still reflect my legal name, this is the name I use in the workplace and the one I would expect to use here.

“Before we conclude, there’s one piece of clarity I want to offer so there are no surprises later.

Professionally, I go by Abby Jane. I’m transgender, and while some materials still reflect my legal name, this is the identity I would be bringing to the role.

I value transparency, and I want to make sure we’re aligned now rather than discovering misalignment down the line.”
