Queer Soup Monthly¶

Idea¶

On a specific day of the month (the second Wednesday; the 17th, etc.) people are invited to bring a soup/chili stew to share in the spirit of queer comraderie. People are encouraged to show up even if they haven't brought anything, but an offer to help clean or set up is appreciated.

You don't have to be queer to participate, but this would be a queer space, so attendees are expected to comport themselves accordingly.

Plan¶

  • Use the Moon Room at Druid City Brewery.
  • People will sign up to bring soups/stews/chilis
  • Others will sign up to bring utensils, napkins, crackers, etc.
  • Some may sign up to help set up or clean up

The evening should be chill. Just eating and talking. I don't think there would be need for a band or entertainment (maybe get the bar to play something appropriate or people can bring vinyl?).

Promotion¶

  • Flyers around the bar and other places where interested people might congregate (The Alcove, Icon, the University Campus).
  • Website for signups
  • Social media posts
  • Word of mouth

Website¶

  • Spec out a Google Forms invitation that has the correct logic
  • Build framework around it
  • Try to find a self-hosted system or one that is GitHub pages friendly

Fundraising¶

  • People can donate to help fund the soup get-together, and/or
  • A local support group for LGBTQ+ kids in need
