# Schedule

5 Days | Tokyo → Kyoto → Hiroshima → Hakone → Tokyo
Birthday = Day 7

Rhythm key:
-  🌸 Soft = decompression, wandering, cafés
- 	🌿 Medium = structured but gentle
- 	🔥 Intense = emotionally or physically heavy anchor

***
## DAY 1 — Arrive Tokyo
🌸 Soft | Decompression Day

- 	Arrive Narita or Haneda
- 	Train into city, hotel check- in
- 	Shower, change
- 	20–40 min neighborhood walk
- 	Convenience- store dinner (onigiri, miso, tea)
- 	Early night

Goal: land safely in your body
