    Schedule¶

    5 Days | Tokyo → Kyoto → Hiroshima → Hakone → Tokyo Birthday = Day 7

    Rhythm key: - 🌸 Soft = decompression, wandering, cafés - 🌿 Medium = structured but gentle - 🔥 Intense = emotionally or physically heavy anchor

    DAY 1 — Arrive Tokyo¶

    🌸 Soft | Decompression Day

    • Arrive Narita or Haneda
    • Train into city, hotel check- in
    • Shower, change
    • 20–40 min neighborhood walk
    • Convenience- store dinner (onigiri, miso, tea)
    • Early night

    Goal: land safely in your body

    DAY 2 — Asakusa + Ueno¶

    🌿 Medium

    • Senso-ji Temple
    • Nakamise shopping street
    • Ueno Park
    • Tokyo National Museum (optional)
    • Coffee + cake stop
    • Dinner near hotel

    DAY 3 — Shibuya + Harajuku + Meiji Shrine¶

    🔥 Intense (Urban Sensory Day)

    • Meiji Jingu forest walk
    • Harajuku wandering
    • Shibuya Crossing (once, reverently)
    • Loft or Tokyu Hands1
    • Daikanyama dinner

    DAY 4 — Museums + Bookstores¶

    🌸 Soft

    • Mori Art Museum
    • Jimbocho used bookstores
    • Stationery stores
    • Café hopping
    • Long bath + skincare night

    DAY 5 — Neighborhood Day (Shimokitazawa or Koenji)¶

    🌿 Medium

    • Vintage shops
    • Indie bookstores
    • Vinyl shops
    • People-watching cafés
    • Dinner at a tiny local place

    DAY 6 — Flex / Rest / Rain Buffer¶

    🌸 Soft

    • Anything you skipped
    • Light shopping
    • Hair or spa
    • Or literally nothing

    DAY 7 — 🎂 Birthday Day in Tokyo¶

    🔥 Intense (But Romantic, Not Heavy)

    (This is your sacred pivot day.)

    • Morning shrine visit (gratitude ritual)
    • Solo gift purchase for yourself
    • Fancy lunch
    • Nezu Museum
    • Sunset river walk
    • High- end dessert café
    • Champagne in your hotel room

    DAY 8 — Tokyo → Kyoto¶

    🌿 Medium (Transit + Arrival)

    • Bullet train to Kyoto
    • Hotel check- in
    • Kamo River walk
    • Dinner in Pontocho
    • Early night

    DAY 9 — Eastern Kyoto Temples¶

    🔥 Intense (Spiritual + Physical)

    • Kiyomizu-dera
    • Sannenzaka / Ninenzaka
    • Yasaka Shrine
    • Tea ceremony
    • Long slow dinner

    DAY 10 — Philosopher’s Path + Ginkaku- ji¶

    🌸 Soft

    • Canal walk
    • Silver Pavilion
    • Bookshop café
    • Journaling afternoon
    • Light dinner

    DAY 11 — Arashiyama¶

    🌿 Medium

    • Bamboo grove (early)
    • Tenryu- ji
    • River walk
    • Monkey park (optional)
    • Afternoon nap

    DAY 12 — Kyoto → Hiroshima¶

    🌿 Medium (Transit + Emotional Setup)

    • Bullet train to Hiroshima
    • Hotel check- in
    • Evening river walk
    • Quiet dinner
    • Early night

    DAY 13 — Hiroshima Peace Day¶

    🔥 Intense (Moral Anchor Day)

    • Peace Memorial Park
    • Atomic Bomb Dome
    • Hiroshima Peace Museum
    • Long silent river walk
    • Simple dinner
    • Early bed

    (You will be emotionally done. This is correct.)

    DAY 14 — Hiroshima → Hakone¶

    🌿 Medium (Transit + Reset)

    • Morning train to Hakone
    • Ryokan check- in
    • Private onsen
    • Kaiseki dinner
    • Early night

    DAY 15 — Hakone → Tokyo → Fly Home¶

    🌸 Soft (Closure Day)

    • Early onsen soak
    • Train to Tokyo
    • Airport train
    • Reflection
    • Cry softly on the plane
