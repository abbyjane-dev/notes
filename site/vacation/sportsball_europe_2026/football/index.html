    Drinks¶

    • London

      • "A pint of lager/bitter please"
      • Cider is acceptable
      • Nothing fussy
      • Card is standard, contactless is universal
      • Cash is accepted but not expected
      • Ordering:
        • go to bar
        • order
        • tap card
        • take pint
        • move
      • No tipping; simply "Cheers"

    • Hamburg

      • "Ein Bier, bitte"
      • Can specify "Astra" or "Holsten"
        • Apfelschorle (apple juice sparkling water)
        • Radler (shandy)
      • Jolly Roger: CASH
      • Clockers: Cash but card is fine
