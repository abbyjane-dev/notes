11th Amendment Repeal¶

Section 1¶

The Eleventh Amendment to the Constitution of the United States is hereby repealed.

Section 2¶

Neither the United States nor any State shall be immune, by reason of sovereignty, from suit in any court of the United States or of any court of any State arising under this Constitution, the laws of the United States, or treaties made under their authority.

Section 3¶

Congress shall have power to regulate the remedies available in such suits, including limitations on damages, prospective relief, and procedural requirements.

Section 4¶

No doctrine of sovereign immunity, whether derived from common law, constitutional interpretation, or judicial construction, shall bar jurisdiction otherwise granted by this Constitution or by law.

Section 5¶

No immunity from suit or liability shall be recognized as a constitutional requirement unless expressly stated in this Constitution.
