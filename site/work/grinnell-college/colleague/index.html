    Colleague¶

    Establish institutional access¶

    Make sure the Registrar’s Office (not just IT) has logins for Ellucian Customer Center and eCommunities.

    Step 2: Embed forum participation¶

    Reference threads and discussions that show an institution solving the same problem. Document what I've learned in a knowledge base

    Step 3: Build outward reputation¶

    Post solutions, not simply questions. Go to Ellucian Live. hehe

    How to phrase it later (gracefully)¶

    If you bring it up in a future meeting, say it like this: “One of the best parts of the Colleague ecosystem is the user community. I’d love for our team to get plugged into the Ellucian forums — other registrars post templates, workflows, and automation ideas there every week. We don’t have to reinvent the wheel.”
