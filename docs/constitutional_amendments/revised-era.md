# Revised Equal Rights Amendment

## Section 1  
The rights, privileges, and immunities secured by this Constitution, as amended, shall not be denied or abridged by the United States or by any State on account of gender.

## Section 2  
Congress shall have power to enforce this article by appropriate legislation. All existing and proposed laws, treaties, and judicial decisions under this Constitution shall be consistent with this article.

## Section 3
For purposes of this article, gender includes sex, gender identity, gender expression, and any classification or treatment based thereon.
