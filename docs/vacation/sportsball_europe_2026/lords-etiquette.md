# Lord's Etiquette

## General

- Inside voice
- Respectful
- Pay attention to the match
- No standing or obstructing sight lines

## Loo/Pint Runs

- After an over, get up, decisively
- Do the business, don't rush, it's cricket
- You will miss an over, that's cool there are many more that day
- If an over is in progress when you get back, linger in the entryway
- Back to your seat, no fuss, no muss
